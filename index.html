SUNDAY WORSHIP SERVICES
Sunday at 10:00 AM

Sunday morning is our main Worship service designed to bring the entire congregation together to praise God, to pray and to partake into the preaching of the Word of God. Our worship team leads the service in the beginning, being joined by our various choirs and musical groups that create a wonderful, God-glorifying time of praise. We spend some time in congregational prayer, sometimes we hear testimonies and then we listen to an inspired message preached by one of our pastors.

BIBLE STUDY
Fridays 7:00 pm - 8:30 pm

Every Friday night we gather for prayer and Bible Study. Our pastors lead Bible study series and help us better understand the Word of God in order to put it into practice in our daily lives.

Prayer Meeting
Wednesday 7:00 pm - 8:30 pm (Zoom)

Wednesday nights we gather virtually on Zoom for a time of prayer and fellowship that helps strengthen our church family and our walk with God.

AWANA
Fridays 7:00 pm - 8:30 pm

The Bethel Awana Club is a place for children from 3 years old all the way to high school to gather on Friday nights to learn Bible lessons, memorize Bible verses and play lots of fun games.

310 South Lemon St.

Bethel Baptist Church is located in beautiful downtown Anaheim. Having been built in 1928, the church building is a historical landmark.

Parking entrance is from Lemon Street and the exit is on Broadway. The entrance and exit for the building is from the inside of the parking lot.
